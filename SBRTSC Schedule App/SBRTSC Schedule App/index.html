About

This Daily Trip Schedule Application was designed to provide a more convenient way for commuters and drivers to obtain the exact time schedule of jeepneys bound from Santa Cruz to Boac and vice versa, as well as the tentative time of the trip, the total carrying capacity of each vehicle, as well as the next and departing vehicles, and whether they are all filled. This application is intended to help PUJs, or Public Utility Jeepneys is to change the traditional way of trip scheduling into a modernized one wherein the commuters can now access the real-time set of trip schedules and the availability of jeepneys through their mobile phones. The development of this application will also improve the quality of service and save a lot of time for commuters. With this organization, it will be more efficient for drivers and commuters to know the latest update on the schedule. We think that the major benefit that comes with using an online application is convenience. Commuters no longer need to wait in line at the dispatcher; instead, they can simply open up an app on their smartphone or tablet device and find out which jeepney will be available for them without waiting. Additionally, our application offers real-time updates that the organization needs, so that the users can now identify what jeepneys are arriving at stops along their routes. The drivers can also access the application by knowing which commuters will arrive, reserve, and what place they should pick up.
